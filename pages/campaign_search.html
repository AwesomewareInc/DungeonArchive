Character conjunction key:

  • A "and/or" B: get messages from A doing the next action and get messages of B doing the next action (or just existing).
  • A "mentioning" B: get messages from A followed directly by B doing the next action (or just existing).
  • A "talking about" B: get messages from A with a certain keyword (B).
  • "in": narrow the search results to a specific area (B); for best results, type the exact name of the Discord channel, without the hashtag.

Each prompt addition narrows the search down more unless it's an and/or chain, which is important to note due to the confusing nature of the and/or chain. It's a bit confusing to explain, but essentially, something like "Poopotron and/or Dr. Poopy Fart mentioning Poopburbia" will only return Poopotron mentioning Poopburbia, and I would have to do some major restructuring to the SearchMessages function to fix this. If you only have a chain of and/or though then this will work just fine.
